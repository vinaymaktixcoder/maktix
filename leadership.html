Leadership Team

Roger Singh

CEO

Roger Singh is working in Logistics Industry from last 15 years. Roger Started Logistics Brokerage Company Called Direct Connect Logistics also known as DCL out of Indianapolis in 2019. Roger built a company that revolutionized the way businesses manage their supply chain operations.

One of the key factors behind his success has been the ability to adapt and leverage technology to optimize logistics processes. Cutting-edge technology platform has empowered DCL in his leadership to efficiently match shippers with carriers, optimize routes, manage capacity, and provide real-time visibility and tracking. This has enabled him to streamline logistics operations, reduce costs, and improve efficiency, resulting in increased customer satisfaction and loyalty.

As the founder of DCL, Roger is immensely proud of his accomplishments and the impact he had made in the logistics industry. His journey from a startup to a multi-million-dollar enterprise has been challenging, but also immensely rewarding. It is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and technology used, as well as the trust and support of valued clients and carriers.

In 2018 Roger Sold his Logistics company to a PE company. Roger believes that the success is a result of our unwavering commitment to core values, focus on customer-centricity, relentless pursuit of excellence and use of right tools to get the job done.

With a proven track record of success and drawing on his extensive experience in logistics and supply chain management Roger is dedicated to help Logistics Industry through MakTix where he is committed to help anyone who is related to Logistics industry by providing right technology and tools to success in their business.
