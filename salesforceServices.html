INTEGRATIONS

Integrating Salesforce within your system can greatly enhance your Lead-to-Revenue process by enabling rapid and efficient data transmission throughout your various systems. With this integration, you can streamline data entry in one system and automatically synchronize the updated information across all your other systems, without the need for manual intervention. At MakTix Solutions, we specialize in customizing the integration process to enable seamless data transfer, starting from lead generation to invoicing, with minimal data entry. We employ a Master Data Management process to ensure that your data has a single source of truth, which helps to reduce the occurrence of bad data across multiple systems. Our approach involves utilizing API technology to integrate your systems seamlessly, enabling smooth and uninterrupted data transmission.

DATA MIGRATION SERVICES

Migrating to a new system can be a complex and expensive undertaking for any organization. At MakTix Solutions, we recognize the challenges associated with this process, and we offer comprehensive data migration services to streamline the transition. Our approach involves utilizing accelerators to consolidate IT systems, improve data quality, and eliminate duplicate data, ultimately enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of your data models. By leveraging a Master Data Management (MDM) approach, we help establish a single system record that becomes the source of truth for your data.
